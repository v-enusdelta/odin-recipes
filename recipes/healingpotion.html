Healing Potion

Description

A slightly bitter, red concotion that heals small wounds, headaches, and exhaustion.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Rogue's Morsels
  • 3 bunches Mergrass
  • 1 C water

Steps

  1. Extract 3 morsels into their salts
  2. Bring 1 cup of water to boil. Steep mergass for five minutes. Add rogue's morsels salts to the pot.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Using a fine-mesh sive or cheesecloth, strain the mixture into a glass bottle.
  5. Use within 3 days. Discard remaining solution.
