Greater Healing Potion

Description

A sweet, red concotion that heals small broken bones, stops bleeding, and restores enough energy for a half day.

Ingredients

  • 3 bunches Balsalm
  • 3 bunches Mergrass
  • 1 C water

Steps

  1. In a clean braiser, set the balsam aflame and let it burn until the whole plant is ash.
  2. Bring 1 cup of water to boil. Steep mergass for five minutes. Add ashes of balsam to the pot.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Using a fine-mesh sive or cheesecloth, strain the mixture into a glass bottle.
  5. Use within 6 days. Discard remaining solution.
