Sleep Potion

Description

A tasteless, thin solution that puts the drinker to sleep for four hours.

Ingredients

  • 3 bunches Waterbloom
  • 3 bunches Lifeleaf
  • 1 C water

Steps

  1. Dry waterbloom and lifeleaf in the sun for two days.
  2. Add dried herbs to an empty ceramic teapot.
  3. Bring 1 cup of water to boil.
  4. Pour over dried herbs and let steep for 20 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into potion bottle. Use within 1 day. Discard the remaining solution.
